KASIPAG N.G.O. ACTIVITIES

KASIPAG N.G.O. provides microfinancing and other services to common people and uplifts their self-esteem by entrusting soft loans with lower interest rates and longer payment period while at the same time involving them and encouraging them to participate in improving the environment.

We help clients realize their aspirations and make them aim for bigger goals while teaching them the value of integrity, cooperation, hard work, planning, saving for the future, and instilling love for Mother Earth — which will eventually help improve their lives.

About Us

MISSION

To provide microfinancing and other services to common people and uplift their self-esteem by entrusting soft loans with lower interest rates and longer payment period and at the same time involve them and encourage them to participate in improving the environment. Help them realize their aspirations and make them aim for bigger goals while teaching them the value of integrity, cooperation, hard work, planning, saving for the future and instill love for Mother Earth which will eventually help improve their lives.

VISION

KASIPAG will be a helping hand and byword in Microfinancing and other NGO Services not only in Luzon but also in other regions in the Philippines while continuing to provide financial assistance and promote food security and environment awareness. Through having worthwhile programs and activities promoting these endeavors and likewise advocate better living conditions for people with integrity, who are hardworking, knowledgeable of sound financial management and values love for Mother Earth.

KASIPAG OR KAtulong Sa Inyong PAGunlad

“To Help, To Create and To Provide”

-A financial assistance for lower interest rates and longer period of payment-

KASIPAG is a corporation started in 2015 as a family business located at Barangay Papaya, San Antonio Nueva Ecija. Currently managed by Ms. Sharon S. Apostol – President together with her youngest brother Mr. Winkle Sulit – Vice President. Kasipag saw that lack of capital was keeping small businesses. Traditional loan was too large, expensive and inaccessible for the people. Kasipag come up with an idea, if the client groups to share a loan and guarantee repayment they could access the funds they need to invest in their small business. The idea provides to be versatile and revolutionary in the urban and rural areas. It was the dawn of KASIPAG Microfinancing, Inc.

KASIPAG has 107 branches already in North and Central Luzon like Albay, Abra, Aurora, Bataan, Batangas, Benguet, Bulacan, Cagayan, Cam Sur, Cavite, Ilocos Norte, Ilocos Sur, Isabela, La Union, Laguna, Nueva Ecija, Nueva Vizcaya, Pampanga, Pangasinan, Quezon, Quirino, Rizal, Tarlac, Sorsogon and Zambales. We will continue to PROVIDE assistance to those who are in need, will CREATE more programs for the progress of the many and also to HELP enhanced and improved their quality of life.
